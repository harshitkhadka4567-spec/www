Bookkeeping

Accounting software support, QuickBooks guidance, POS support, and full-charge bookkeeping services.

QuickBooks & Accounting Software

Business Brains is proud to be a QuickBooks Professional Advisor since 1999. We also support other small business accounting software, including Xero, Sage, and Manager.io.

For Point-of-Sales systems, we primarily support Small business accounting software(on oremise or cloud based). Other industry-specific Point-of-Sales systems may be supported on a per-need basis.

Integrated Business Support

Nitin Thakkar, being an experienced accountant, has the added advantage of knowing how to integrate IT, financial, and POS systems.

Clients can receive timely reports and concentrate on their business, while their accountant has complete information at the end of the year with minimum difficulty.

Troubleshooting, Training & Transition

Business Brains troubleshoots user issues efficiently. Businesses may also receive a customized manual, on request, to help guide users through questions that arise.

Experience and knowledge help provide a smooth transition to QuickBooks, whether a business is using QuickBooks for the first time, switching from Excel spreadsheets, moving from shoe-box based accounting systems, or migrating from older accounting software.

Business Brains installs and sets up small business accounting software (on cloud or on premise based), trains personnel, and provides ongoing support.

Full-Charge Bookkeeping

Business Brains also provides full-charge bookkeeping services, including:

  • General ledger and sub-ledger maintenance
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Tax accountant-ready financial statements
  • Year-end bookkeeping support for tax preparation
